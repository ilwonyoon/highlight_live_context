## Context

Highlight for Teams is reimagining how knowledge workers interact with AI by building a contextual memory layer that understands what you're working on in real-time. Instead of manually feeding context to AI, Highlight passively captures screen activity, audio, clipboard, and other signals to build "Living Context" – a dynamic understanding of your work that powers proactive assistance.

**Core product principles:**

- **Ambient intelligence:** Highlight learns from your work without requiring manual input
- **Contextual awareness:** The AI knows what you're doing, what you've done, and what you need next
- **Respectful automation:** Users maintain control over privacy, data collection, and when AI intervenes
- **Craft & polish:** Every interaction should feel considered, delightful, and invisible when not needed

---

## The Challenge

### Problem Statement

Users need transparency and control over what Highlight "remembers" – but exposing raw data (screenshots, transcripts, clipboard history) creates cognitive overload and breaks the magic of ambient intelligence.

**Your task:** Design a Desktop "Memory" interface that allows users to **edit and curate their memory** — Delete sensitive moments, annotate important context, or flag items as "always remember this"

**Constraints:**

- This feature must work for both power users (who want granular control) and casual users (who just want peace of mind)
- Consider how this scales: users may have thousands of memory items over time
- **Do not** rely on the existing visual style of Highlight. **Come up with your own!**
